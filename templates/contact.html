Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

+

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our support line directly for immediate assistance.

How can I schedule a demonstration of your learning platform?

+

To schedule a demonstration, simply fill out our contact form with your request and preferred dates/times. Our sales team will reach out to confirm your appointment and provide you with all necessary details.

Do you offer technical support for students?

+

Yes, we provide technical support for all registered students. Support is available Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM and on weekends from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Students can access support through their learning dashboard or by emailing support@learnnexus.com.

What are your partnership opportunities?

+

We offer various partnership opportunities including content partnerships, technology integrations, and educational institution collaborations. Please contact our partnerships team at partners@learnnexus.com for more information about our partnership programs.
